Morocco's cannabis regulatory agency has reported figures from a second harvest of legally cultivated cannabis by licensed cooperatives in designated provinces, according to an agency statement.

## The figures

The statement lists the number of registered farmers, cultivated area, and cooperatives participating in the licensed program. Officials said the program is limited to specified provinces and regulated end uses.

## Farmer participation

Cooperative representatives quoted by national media described the registration process and pricing arrangements. The agency said licensed output is directed toward regulated industrial and export channels.

The regulator said it would continue reporting on registrations and processing capacity as the program develops.
