Thai health authorities have advanced a bill intended to consolidate cannabis rules, which currently exist across multiple ministerial notices, into a single act, according to government documents.

## The consolidation effort

Officials quoted in the documents said a unified law would clarify licensing for cultivation, retail, and medical supply. The draft describes registration requirements and oversight responsibilities.

## Sector reaction

Business associations cited by national media said clearer rules would help operators plan, while some raised questions about transition provisions for existing license holders. Public-health commentators submitted feedback summarized in the consultation record.

The ministry said the bill would proceed through the normal legislative stages and that details could change during review.
