Malta's cannabis authority has released updated figures on the number of licensed non-profit associations operating under the country's regulated model, according to a report on the authority's website.

## The numbers

The report lists totals for registered associations and describes compliance checks carried out during the reporting period. Officials quoted in the document said inspections focus on record-keeping and membership rules.

## How the model works

Under Malta's framework, associations may supply registered members within set limits and are barred from advertising, the authority's guidance states. The report notes that commercial retail is not part of the model.

The authority said it would continue publishing periodic updates on registrations and enforcement actions.
