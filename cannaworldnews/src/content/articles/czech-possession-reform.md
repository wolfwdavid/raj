Legislators in the Czech Republic are debating a bill that would revise the thresholds distinguishing administrative fines from criminal penalties for cannabis possession, according to parliamentary records.

## The proposal

The draft text sets specific quantities and describes how enforcement would categorize cases. Sponsors quoted in committee minutes said the goal is clearer and more consistent handling.

## Points of contention

Opposition members cited in the record raised questions about how the thresholds were chosen and about coordination with neighboring countries. Public-health officials submitted written comments that the committee summarized in its notes.

A vote timeline has not been finalized, the records indicate. Analysts told regional outlets the bill's path depends on committee amendments still under discussion.
