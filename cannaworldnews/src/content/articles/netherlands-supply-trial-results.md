Researchers evaluating the Netherlands' controlled-supply experiment have published an interim report describing how participating coffeeshops sourced products through a regulated chain, according to the study team.

## Scope of the trial

The experiment covers a set of municipalities and licensed growers operating under government oversight. The interim report summarizes logistics, product tracking, and participating businesses' operational feedback.

> "The interim data is descriptive; conclusions come with the final report," the research team wrote.

## Next steps

The team said a final evaluation is planned once the trial period concludes. Municipal officials cited in local media said day-to-day operations continued without major disruption during the reporting window.

The report stops short of policy recommendations, noting that those are for evaluators and lawmakers to weigh later.
