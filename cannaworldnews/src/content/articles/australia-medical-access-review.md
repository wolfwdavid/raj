Australia's therapeutic-goods regulator has opened a consultation on the pathways prescribers use to authorize medical cannabis products, according to a notice on the agency's website.

## The consultation

The document asks stakeholders about the current authorization pathways, record-keeping, and product categories. Officials quoted in the notice framed the review as periodic oversight of an existing scheme.

## Stakeholder input

Medical bodies and patient organizations are expected to file submissions, according to trade press. The regulator emphasized that the review concerns process and administration within the established medical framework.

A summary of submissions will be published before any changes are proposed, the notice said.
